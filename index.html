About Me

Hi there! I'm Marouane, a computer science chameleon—part systems engineer, part creative freelancer. My journey started with fixing hardware and battling network outages as an IT Technician at Kharkov National University, where I learned that turning it off and on again actually works 90% of the time.

Currently, I'm navigating the deep waters of the 42 Network, mastering low-level C, tackling algorithms, and aiming for the stars in AI development. I love diving into the logic behind the code and building things that actually solve problems.

On the flip side, I have a creative streak. I freelance on Fiverr, juggling everything from video editing and ad creation to crafting the perfect resume. I believe versatile skills make for a versatile developer.
